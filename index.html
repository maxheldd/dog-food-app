Safety Notes

Why PupPlate says yes, no, or be careful.

The app rewards clearly identified, dog-appropriate proteins and useful nutrients, and it warns hard on toxic foods, ingredient vagueness, heavy sugar, and known risk items.

Usually good signs

Plain chicken, turkey, salmon, pumpkin, brown rice, oats, carrots, blueberries, and salmon oil often support a balanced diet.

Usually caution signs

Cheese, bacon, salty sauces, artificial colors, sugar-heavy ingredients, and vague by-products may be less ideal or too rich.

Usually danger signs

Chocolate, xylitol, grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, and macadamia nuts are major problems and should never be treated casually.
