Helpful Notes

What PupPlate treats as green flags and red flags.

The checker rewards named proteins and helpful nutrients, and it warns on toxic foods, vague fillers, heavy sugar, and suspicious additives.

Usually positive

Named meat or fish, salmon oil, pumpkin, oats, sweet potato, probiotics, and limited clean ingredient lists.

Use caution

Meat by-products, artificial colors, corn syrup, excessive sodium, vague “animal fat,” and long filler-heavy labels.

Usually avoid

Chocolate, xylitol, grapes, raisins, onions, garlic-heavy products, macadamia nuts, and anything that causes clear symptoms in your dog.
